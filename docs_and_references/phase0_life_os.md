## Weekend / Light Day Schedule

| Time | Block |
|------|-------|
| 8:00–9:00 | Wake + breakfast + wind up |
| 9:00–11:00 | Study block (2hr) |
| 11:00 onwards | Free — family, gf, chores, whatever life brings |
| 10:00–10:15 | Planning |

**Rule:** Get the 2 hours done in the morning before the day gets away from you. Once it's done, it's done — no guilt, no "finding time later."  
**Workout:** Rest day on weekends. No tracking pressure.

---

## Habit Floors

These are the bare minimums that keep the streak alive on depleted or chaotic days. The goal on bad days is "don't break the chain," not "perform."

| Habit | Target | Floor |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Study | 7.5hr (weekday) / 2hr (weekend) | 2hr any day |
| Workout | Full session (weekdays) | 20 min, any movement |
| Reading / deliberate consumption | 1 session | 1 page / 1 intentional thing |

**Fried-day fallback (pre-decided, never negotiated on the day):**  
Floors only — 2hr light study, no workout pressure, one page reading.

---

## Daily Planning Note (Tonight-Only View)

Happens at 10:00–10:15. Three sections, nothing more:

1. **Top 3 tasks for tomorrow** — not a full list, just 3
2. **Energy tag** — one word: *high / medium / low*
3. **Fried-day fallback** — already decided above; just remind yourself it exists

**Rule:** Never build a visible accusing backlog. Pull from a parking lot. Today's note only.

## Streak Tracker

Three habits tracked as simple yes/no per day:

- 📚 **Studied** — hit the floor minimum
- 💪 **Worked out** — or rest day on weekends (auto-pass)
- 🎬 **Consumed deliberately** — book, worthwhile show/film, not brainrot scroll

**Implementation:** Deferred to Phase 1 Obsidian + Claude setup. Will be lightweight.

---

## Sunday Review (15-min hard cap)

Same five questions every week. Answer in 2–3 sentences max. No elaboration.

1. **Did the floors hold?** (study, workout, reading — yes/no)
2. **What actually drained me this week?**
3. **What worked better than expected?**
4. **What's the one thing I'd change next week?**
5. **Am I on track with my main goal?** (cert progress or current focus)
